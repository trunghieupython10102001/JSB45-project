    WE PRIDE IN OUR HISTORY

    "Bowl of Phở" is a family-owned Vietnamese restaurant opened since 1980 by a Vietnamese family. Here, we pride ourselves in our passion and tradition in cooking soulful food to enjoy at any time of the day. Our cooking recipes are family-owned, and have been passed down from many familial generations. Food quality is one of many aspects we pride ourselves in. Meat, foul, and fish are all locally sourced, vegetables and fruits are all hand-picked from local farms, because we support the financial growth of local owned business. Here at "Bowl of Phở," you can always expect the best food quality while enjoy the fresh taste of Vietnamese traditional food.

    CUSTOMER'S FAVORITE

    Bún Chả

    A favorite dish among locals and tourists when coming to Vietnam. With tender meat grilled on charcoal, fish sauce diluted with vinegar, water and spices, combining with rice vermicelli, you will always leave your belly full.

    Bánh Mì

    A convenient dish that is fit for any meal of the day. With optional fillings, you can enjoy a customizable loaf of Bánh Mì with creative toppings fit for your own personal taste.

    Phở

    A classic soup noodles dish that brought the flavor of Vietnam to the world. This meal can be enjoyed at any occasion with different versions such as Phở Bò (Beef), Phở Gà (Chicken), Phở Chay (Vegeterian), and many more.
